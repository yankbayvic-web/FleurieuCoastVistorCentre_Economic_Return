Economic Impact of VIC Advice — How This Is Calculated
Economic impact figures are calculated as Total Visitors × $218.63 — the verified unplanned additional spend per person per visit from the SA Accredited Visitor Information Centre Network Survey (February 2023, n=2,195 across 41 VICs). This is money visitors confirmed they would not have spent without advice received at a Visitor Information Centre — making it a direct, evidence-based measure of the economic impact generated by the Fleurieu Coast VIC. A further 51.25% of respondents indicated they would stay longer and spend more following their VIC visit.
